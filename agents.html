Meet the Constellation

A diverse ecosystem of specialized agents designed to support every aspect of the healthcare journey.

Chronic Care Management

Supporting patients with long-term conditions through coaching, reminders, and coordination.

Diabetes Management

Supports patients with lifestyle coaching, medication adherence, and lab test reminders.

Heart Failure Care

Educates on low-sodium diets, monitors weight, and supports medication adherence.

Chronic Kidney Disease

Assists CKD patients with diet education, medication reviews, and lab scheduling.

Pernicious Anemia

Discusses diagnosis, treatment options, and ongoing management strategies.

Screening & Preventative Care

Outreach agents focused on improving health outcomes and quality measures.

Colorectal Cancer Screening

Conducts outreach for FIT or colonoscopy scheduling to increase return rates.

Mammogram Outreach

Encourages patients to complete screenings, answers questions, and assists with scheduling.

Flu Vaccination

Runs seasonal campaigns to remind and schedule patients for flu shots.

Annual Wellness Visit

Identifies eligibility, schedules visits, performs intake, and addresses barriers to access.

Post-Discharge & Recovery

Monitoring patients after facility discharge to prevent readmission.

Pneumonia Recovery

Monitors symptoms, ensures antibiotic adherence, and reviews inhaler use.

Joint Replacement Support

Reviews mobility progress, pain management, and therapy compliance.

Lower Extremity Discharge

Conducts medication reviews and care coordination following discharge.

Patient Access & Operations

Streamlining administrative tasks to improve access.

Appointment Scheduling

Assists patients with scheduling, confirming details, and pre-visit paperwork.

Patient Intake History

Collects medication lists, allergies, symptoms, and visit reasons prior to appointments.

Social Determinants & Community

Addressing broader health and safety needs.

SDOH Navigation

Assesses needs regarding food, housing, and safety, connecting patients to resources.

Community Assessment

Identifies unmet health and social needs within a community to guide support.

Specialized & Utility Agents

Innovative solutions for specific healthcare challenges.

ProviderSync AI

The Problem

Doctor contact information is often incorrect in directories.

The Solution

Multi-agent system updates directories using NPI, state board, and web search data.

LabMate

The Problem

Patients struggle to understand complex lab reports.

The Solution

Generates unique QR codes for interactive, explained lab reports.

Audio Prescription

The Problem

Handwritten prescriptions are hard to read and share.

The Solution

Digital prescriptions with QR codes for easy pharmacist and patient access.

Personal Health Insight

The Problem

Deriving insights from wearable data requires complex reasoning.

The Solution

Chatbot for users and doctors to derive behavioral intelligence from wearable data.

Specialist Report Agents

The Problem

General doctors need specialist expertise for specific images.

The Solution

Dedicated agents for Retinal, Dermatology, Radiology, and Pathology image analysis.

AI Clinical Decision Support

The Problem

Lack of real-time support during tele-health consults.

The Solution

Suggests next questions and differential diagnoses in real-time.

Pharma Bot AI

The Problem

Patients have questions about their specific prescriptions.

The Solution

Answers medication questions based on the specific prescription context.

Evo 2

The Problem

Predicting future diseases is difficult without genetic context.

The Solution

Analyzes sequenced genetic data to report on potential future health risks.

NotebookLM Discharge

The Problem

Patients forget generic discharge instructions.

The Solution

Generates personalized, condition-specific discharge instructions.

AI Companions

The Problem

Manual data entry burden for Asha workers and Nurses.

The Solution

Voice-enabled technology to automate data entry and documentation.
