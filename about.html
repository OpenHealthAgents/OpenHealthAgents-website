Revolutionizing Healthcare with AI Agents

Making AI accessible, affordable, and seamless for healthcare professionals worldwide.

Our Origins

Open Health Agents is an initiative by Health Innovators Inc, a non-profit organization located in Boston, Massachusetts operating since 2013 (EIN # 46-3424042-990), and Aarya Foundation Inc, a non-Governmental Organization located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India (Darpan ID # TS/2025/0502385).

Mission Statement

To revolutionize healthcare by providing an AI-powered agents app store that enhances clinical decision-making, automates administrative tasks, and improves patient outcomes — making AI accessible, affordable, and seamless for healthcare professionals worldwide.

Vision Statement

To become the world’s leading marketplace for AI-driven healthcare solutions, empowering hospitals, clinics, and developers to leverage cutting-edge AI technology for better, faster, and more efficient medical care.

The Problem

The healthcare industry faces several critical challenges.

Administrative Overload

Doctors spend 30-50% of their time on documentation instead of patient care.

Medical Errors

Lack of AI-powered decision support leads to misdiagnoses and treatment inefficiencies.

Data Fragmentation

Interoperability issues hinder seamless integration of AI solutions in healthcare systems.

High Costs

Developing and deploying AI solutions for hospitals is expensive and resource-intensive.

Lack of AI Accessibility

Many healthcare professionals lack the technical expertise to implement AI effectively.

Our Solution: The AI Agents App Store

A one-stop marketplace for ready-to-use AI-powered healthcare agents.

Pre-Built AI Agents

Medical transcription, clinical decision support, radiology analysis, and more.

Seamless Integration

Works with EHR systems (Epic, Cerner), FHIR, and HL7.

Regulatory Compliance

HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC2-compliant AI models.

Plug-and-Play Access

No technical expertise needed for deployment.

How It Works

  1. Browse & Discover

    Explore a diverse range of AI agents designed for clinical decision support, medical transcription, patient engagement, radiology image analysis, and more.

  2. Deploy & Integrate

    Seamlessly integrate AI agents into hospital systems, electronic health records (EHRs), and telehealth platforms.

  3. Customize & Optimize

    Tailor AI agents to meet the specific needs of your healthcare organization.

Key Features

Comprehensive AI solutions for every aspect of healthcare.

Clinical AI Agents

  • AI-powered Clinical Decision Support for doctors & nurses.
  • Medical Transcription Agents that convert speech to structured medical notes.
  • Radiology AI Agents for automated X-ray, MRI, and CT scan analysis.

Telehealth Assistants

  • AI Virtual Health Assistants for real-time patient triage.
  • Automated Consultation Summaries for healthcare providers.
  • Multilingual AI Agents for improved accessibility.

Admin & Workflow

  • AI-powered Insurance Claim Processing and billing automation.
  • Smart AI Chatbots for appointment scheduling and patient reminders.
  • AI-driven Revenue Cycle Management for hospitals.

Medical Research

  • AI models trained on medical literature for drug discovery and clinical trials.
  • AI-powered Patent Search & RAG-based Scientific Knowledge Retrieval.
